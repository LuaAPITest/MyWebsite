Now that you picked out your CPU and GPU you will need a few more things before we get to building your gaming rig!

To begin with, you will need a good ATX form factor motherboard that supports your CPU and the RAM you picked we recommend going with Two 8 gigs sticks of DDR4 or DDR5 memory (RAM - Random Access Memory) making a 16 gigs with 3200mhz or higher memory for your PC.

Here are some of our favorite motherboard and RAM sticks

You will also need storage (SSD and/or HDD) we recommend going with SSD since it's significantly faster than HDD and we recommend getting at least 1 gig or more of M.2 PCI4 or later storage stick. Lastly, you will need a few more simple things like a good mid-tower case that fits the GPU you picked earlier and that has good airflow, you will also need a power supply unit that has enough watts to power your gaming rig we recommend getting an 80+ Gold and a full modular for easier upgrades later on, a a couple of fans to keep your PC cooled we recommend also getting an AIO liquid cooler for high-end CPUs or a Noctua NH-D15 for a good air cooler and most importantly an Operating system (a version of Windows) and gaming peripherals.

For a easy pick on those stuff head over to our gaming peripherals/parts page here.

Now that you got all the parts needed you can head over to PC Build Walkthrough for extra help with putting the build together or you can watch this video from TechSource that also shows you how to get into your BIOAS settings and speed up your RAM.
